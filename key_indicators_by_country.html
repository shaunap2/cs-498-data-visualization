At this point the viewer is invited to interact with the data to explore key indicators for specific countries of interest. For each country the total number of deaths, damage in millions of dollars, and total homes destroyed aggregated per year is displayed. Selecting a country from the drop down enables understanding how earthquakes have impacted each country over time since 1900.

Have fun exploring!
